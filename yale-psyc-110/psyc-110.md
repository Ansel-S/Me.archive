## Foundations

### The Neuron

As Lewis Carroll's Alice might have phrased it,
> You're nothing but a pack of neurons.

**All-or-None Law**: Much like binary code in computing, a neuron either fires a full electrical impulse or it doesn't fire at all.

**Neurotransmitters**: Chemical messengers released into the synapse to communicate between cells.

### Brain Structure

- **Subcortical Structures**: Basic instincts
- **The Cerebal Cortex**: (*E.g. reasoning, language, perception*)
- **Hemispheric Specialization**: The left brain focuses on logic and language, while the right brain focuses on spatial and holistic perception.
- **Split-brain**: Visual field and movements are not coordinated.
